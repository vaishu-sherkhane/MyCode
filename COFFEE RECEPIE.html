COFFEE RECIPE

INGREDENTS

  • milk
  • sugar
  • tea powder
  • water

DIRECTIONS

  1. first boil water in a bowl.
  2. secondly add sugar and tea powder in it and boil it on a meadium flame for 3-4 minutes.
  3. after some time add milk in it over a medium flame upto 6-7 minutes. until we get bubbles. Then we observe color changes of white milk into brown shade. when it is ready.
  4. lastly, OUR TEA IS READY TO DRINK.
